Employer

The Cooperators was started by a group of farmers in 1945 in an attempt to support their local community through hard times when traditional insurers would not meet their needs. Today the Cooperators serves over a million clients across the country. It was started by farmers who had lost everything to the great depression and realize that traditional companies can not be trusted. This formed the cooperative business model.
