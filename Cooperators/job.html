Job Posting

The Co-operators Information Technology team aspires to be a leader in applying technology to power
business strategies. We connect concepts with solutions to create value and efficiencies for our clients, employees, and communities.
Our success is driven by our skilled and diverse team who are passionate about excellence, innovation, and agility.

Our DevOps team is looking for talented people to help enable our solution delivery teams.
As part of the DevOps team, you will be at the forefront of innovation and automation. We embrace new solutions, technologies and processes.

We're looking for individuals who want to apply creativity and experience to shape the future of our solutions. You get to join a team that makes an impact.
We challenge each other to work hard, always improve and learn, and ask hard questions. You get to be part of something unique and challenging. You will
be supporting the delivery of amazing products.

What you can expect?
· A whole lot of learning
· New technologies and emerging best practices
· Exposure to our business partners

Job Responsibilities
Some of the things you will work on
· Use DevOps related tools to support our development teams: Jenkins, Bitbucket (GIT), Cloud Platforms (AWS, Azure, IBM), System Configuration (Docker, Kubernetes), Support Developer Tools/Build Frameworks (.NET, mongoDB, npm), Splunk
· Help support our Enterprise DevOps suite: JIRA, Confluence, BitBucket, Jenkins, Artifactory
· Gather and translate requirements from Users to develop build, test, package and deploy processes
· Anticipate, identify, and resolve issues and risks affecting own work and help other team members with their issues
· Assist team members in the preparation of program structure, specifications, diagrams and coding logic

Required Skills
On your first day, we'll expect you to have:
· Are currently enrolled in an university, accredited co-op or internship program in Computer Science, Engineering or Math or are currently enrolled in a Masters degree program in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or Math
· Software development experience: Show us what you've done!
· Are fearless, willing to try something new, make mistakes and retry to develop amazing solutions
· Have strong analytical, conceptualization and problem solving skills
· Know how to collaborate and partner with others to deliver on goals
· Good communication skills.
· Solid documentation and source code management skills. You will leave building blocks for future Developers to expand upon your work

Great, but not required if you:
· Familiar with:
· Unix systems
· java, python, Gunit, bash, gradle, selenium, SQL development,
· Experience working on an agile team
· are a DevOps legend

Job

This Winter I was tasked with creating a "scheduler" like script in python to automate our deploy
processes in preparation for the Cooperators moving to cloud server deploys. This process and app development
was rather painful as most test environments were not suitable for actually testing these deploys and had to be
fixed before we could test anything

The python script involved calling various bash scripts when givin a list of deploy steps, while verifying steps
were executed successfully. This required a lot of bash script modification, and due to the nature of the test environment
most testing ended up happing in the last 2 weeks of my co-op

I was the sole contributor to this python script, which ended up being one of the main outputs of our team by the end
of my term as most of my team was pre-occupied with fielding requests from other teams to fix their environments
This left me with a lot of freedom but also not as much guidance as I would have liked
I also made and managed a JIRA service desk for my team so we could field all unplanned work in a way that was
easier to track.

My biggest challenge this term was how to budget time and tasks so I had something to do when waiting for
a dependency to be completed. This involved prioritizing tasks that result in the longest wait before others.
The skills gained from this hurdle will definitely help me in school and later into my career, as time managements
is a great skill to have
