Conclusion

In conclusion, this Cooperators work term has really helped me realize the importance of
requirements gathering and proper test environments. During the term when I started coding this
script, I didnt have much direction in terms of what was required of it. If I took the time at the
beginning of the semester to go over what was needed from it, I wouldn't have had to do as much
refactoring later on. As for the proper test environments, I felt a lot of my progress was inhibited
by the lack of functioning test environments, leaving most of whether or not my program was working to
guess work.

While it seems like I'm casting it in a negative light, I did enjoy my time at the Cooperators. My team was
great and put a lot of trust in me as a developer which was nice. I also got to learn Python, while not the
hardest language to learn, will be very useful for my future in software
