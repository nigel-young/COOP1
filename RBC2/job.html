What will you do?
Create and review technical design documents related to small projects and understanding how the design will be used in the code development process

Participate in walkthrough reviews of technical specifications, programs and unit test plans
Provide estimates on activities/tasks required to perform design, coding and unit testing, improving accuracy over time
Performance tuning to improve performance of single function
Low level planning of work packages meeting project objectives within budget and on time

Facilitate small group meetings for technical design, problem solving and task implementation

This summer I was working with the Core Service Integration team. The team that
I was apart of was responsible for creating new API's and connecting them to back end services

Most of the work I did this summer was related to devops tasks. I was working on a pipeline that allowed
for a more streamlined application deployment. This involved the use of JenkinsFiles included in project directories
that made projects autodeploy on a codebase change or if a merge was made in VCS. Most of this work was done
through Groovy scripts and interacting with various API's to acquire data needed for some of the deploy processes.
I learned a lot of the skills necessary for this job from my last work term at the Cooperators.

The most challenging part about this work term was maintaining a good work ethic while working from home.
Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, the entirety of my co-op work term was done online. This made it difficult
to separate my home life from my work life. It was very difficult to stay on task and I had to implement a
number of strategies to try and separate those parts of my life. At the end of my term I think I had a better
