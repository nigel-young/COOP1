Conclusion

In conclusion, my term at RBC really helped me understand the importance of networking and putting
myself out there in a professional sense. I was able to shadow a TSA working on Springboot RESTful
web services and cement a position at RBC for the next semester.


This work term had my improve my work ethic as well as my ability to learn remotely.
This will be very helpful for the school semester as well as my next work term, as I am hoping
to avoid the commute to Toronto and work from home as much as I can. I was also able to
learn more about jenkins pipelines, which while useful, I hope to move away from in future
work (Devops isn't really my thing).
