Conclusion

taking initiative. I was incredibly lucky that my manager was such a great person
who really wanted me to learn and grow as a worker. Allowing me to find my own place in
the team really allowed for me to get the most I could out of this work term.


This work term helped me improve as a teamate as well as a programmer. I learned so much

and will be able to take that experience with me to my next work term
